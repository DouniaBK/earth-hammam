Earth Hammam Facility

Earth Hammam wellness and spa facility is inspired by the North African landscape, culture and earthy hued colors. Woven through that is serene wellness, bath soaks, stream and treatments rooms, where ancient Moroccan healing traditions take on a modern twist.
